FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

We’ve gathered a few common questions from retailers and brand partners. Have a different question? Let’s talk.

Do you work directly with brands?

Yes. Retail Stride partners directly with brands and authorized distributors to ensure every product is authentic, compliant, and responsibly represented.

Can I buy products without a retail business license?

Not at this time. Retail Stride is a B2B platform serving licensed retail businesses and verified resale partners.

How do you ensure pricing consistency?

We maintain strict MAP compliance and only source from authorized suppliers—ensuring stable pricing across all retail channels.

Can brands apply to join the platform?

Absolutely. We’re always expanding our catalog with premium, compliant brands seeking controlled retail reach.

Do you ship directly to stores?

Yes. We support direct store delivery (DSD) and FBA prep shipments through approved logistics partners.

Is there a membership or access fee?

Access to browse is free for approved retailers. Brand and wholesale partnerships are structured individually.
